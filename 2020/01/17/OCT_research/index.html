Discriminant model for retinal tomography images

Swell Mai
2020-01-17

Introduction

This research explores the neural network and computer vision methods to help determine the diseases of the retinal tomography image acquired by OCT (Optical coherence tomography). Due to the compact and complex structures near the retina, hopefully, we can build a data-driven model to assist medical workers and doctors in distinguishing and preventing the occurrence of fundus diseases. The work can be further extended to include the classification of tissues surrounding the retina. The research is under the supervision of Professor Mingchen Gao.

Requirements

Tensorflow, Keras, Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) images
