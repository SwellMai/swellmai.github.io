Few-shot Feature Space Learning for Congenital Retinal Diseases Recognition

Swell Mai
2020-01-17 (Updated: 2020-06-30)

Introduction

  • Goal
    Recognizing rare hereditary retinal diseases, such as inherited macular dystrophies, based on Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) images, whose main manifestation is the confusion and adhesion of the layers of the retina.

  • Challenge

    1. Number of samples is limited, 26 for abnormal, 32 for normal.
    2. The distinction between the two categories is very cramped and hard to distinguish, even for a human who does not have any related-field experience.
    3. Too much interference from the scan machine.

Requirements

  • Pytorch
  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) images

OCT image

  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) is a non-contact, non-invasive 3-D imaging technique which performs optical sectioning at microscopic resolution. It was commercially introduced to ophthalmology in 1996, and has been widely adopted as the standard for clinical care in identifying the presence of various ocular pathologies.
  • This technology measures the optical back scattering of the tissues of the eye, making it possible to visualize intraocular structures such as the retina and the optic nerve head.
  • The cross-section image we used is from z-x axises.

Pre-processing

(a) Input original image.
(b) Block-matching and 3D filtering (BM3D) for subsequent steps to better capture the structure of the retina.
(c) Strip the location and morphology of the tissue from background.
(d) Further reduces the noise area within the tissue.
(e) Finalize the noise cleanup inside and outside the tissue area and create the mask.
(f) Represent the degree of curvature of the tissue area.
(g) Adjust the input image.
(h) Adjust the mask.
(i) Crop the target area on input by the mask

Siamese Network + Triplet Loss

  • The advantage of Siamese network is that it dilutes the labels and makes the network extensible and have the ability to classify the untrained categories. In addition, this algorithm is also applicable to some small data volume data sets.

  • In our experiments, the Siamese network consists of three share-weight edembedded ResNet to process the input. The Our goal of Siamese network is to output a feature y from a x which will describe later by the embedded neural network inside. Meanwhile, the purpose of training the siamese network is to allow the input image to project the feature map to different locations in the same embedding space space through the loss function instead of directly classifying.
